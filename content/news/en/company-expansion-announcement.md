# ARIS Announces Major Expansion: New Offices and Global Growth Strategy

We're excited to announce a significant expansion of ARIS Vietnam's operations, marking a new chapter in our journey to become a leading technology solutions provider in the Asia-Pacific region.

### Ho Chi Minh City Headquarters Enhancement
Our flagship office in Ho Chi Minh City will undergo a major transformation:

- **Expanded Facilities**: Tripling our office space to accommodate 200+ employees
- **Innovation Labs**: New dedicated spaces for R&D and prototyping
- **Client Experience Centers**: Modern meeting rooms and demonstration areas
- **Employee Amenities**: Enhanced wellness and collaboration spaces

### New Regional Offices
We're establishing new offices in key markets:

#### Hanoi Technology Hub
- **Location**: Prime district in Hanoi
- **Focus**: Government and enterprise solutions
- **Capacity**: 80+ professionals
- **Specialization**: Public sector digital transformation

#### Da Nang Innovation Center
- **Location**: Da Nang IT Park
- **Focus**: Software development and AI research
- **Capacity**: 60+ specialists
- **Specialization**: Emerging technologies and startups

#### International Presence
- **Singapore Office**: Regional headquarters for Southeast Asia
- **Tokyo Satellite**: Partnership development and market expansion
- **Sydney Office**: ANZ market development
