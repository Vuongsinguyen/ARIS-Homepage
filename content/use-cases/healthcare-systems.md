# Healthcare Management Platform

## Challenge
The healthcare provider needed to modernize patient care delivery while maintaining strict regulatory compliance.

## Solution
We built a comprehensive healthcare platform with:
- HIPAA-compliant telemedicine
- Electronic health records (EHR)
- Patient portal and mobile app
- AI-powered diagnostic assistance

## Results
- 10,000+ patients served monthly
- 70% reduction in administrative time
- 95% patient satisfaction rate
- Full regulatory compliance maintained
